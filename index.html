About

$SAFEASS is programmed to reward holders while increasing in both liquidity and value.


It does this by applying a

8.5% tax on transactions
1% goes to holders (instanly and gaslessly)
7.5% burned (forever)
After 156 cycles, transaction fees set to zero and supply stays 550k


Forever Deflationary supply/burn

We Burn 7% of each transaction of SAFEASS, sending it to dead safe address; as address also participaes in the protocol, it accumulates more token, thereby effectively removing them for circulation, increasing the scarcity of SAFEASS.


156 Unique Cycles

Every 1 million token transfer/buys/sells - increases the fee by 0.5%, upto a max 15.5%. On the next 1 million token transactions, the fee resets to 7.5%, completing a cycle. After 156 cycles, the transaction fees are set to zero and supply forever stays 550k.


Dump Protection

You can only sell 1% of the current supply and the sells are ONLY activated when 2 buys of SAFEASS are completed.
